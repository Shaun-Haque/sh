About Me

I'm a rising junior in high school and I love to code. I organize hackathons with Young Hackers and do Web, iOS,
and Python development. I'm a graduate of All Star Code and the founder and president of HEC - The Heritage Engineering Club at my high school. Lack of access to technological resources severely debilitates one's ability to succeed in a world where technology influences human interactions, edcation and entertainment. I know and am currently working to prove with HEC is that one of the most efficient ways to combat this is through peer-to peer-collaboration. Programming gives people the tools to create their dreams and I really enjoy the fact that what you can create is limited only by what you can conceive. I want to use technology in a humanitarian way and I want to give back to those who are disadvantaged due to a lack of technology in their lives.

I'm not just a coder; one of my many hobbies outside of technology is basketball. Basketball promotes the communication skills through the emphasis on teamwork. As someone wwho is great at working in and motivating teams, I hope to use this very concept of active collaboration to create technology as I continue to expand my skills.
